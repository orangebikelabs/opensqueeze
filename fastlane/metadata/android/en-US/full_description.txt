<p><i>Open Squeeze</i> is an Open Source fork of the commercial Android app <a href='https://orangebikelabs.com/products/orangesqueeze/' target='_blank' rel='nofollow'>Orange Squeeze</a> from Orange Bike Labs. Orange Squeeze is an Android app that can be used to control <a href='https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Squeezebox_(network_music_player)' target='_blank'>Logitech Squeezebox</a> devices on the <a href='https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Logitech_Media_Server' target='_blank'>Logitech Media Server</a> platform.</p><p><i>Open Squeeze</i> is a <i>very</i> bad example of how a modern Android app should be developed. It has a decade of legacy code/cruft and has survived to this day despite the very schizophrenic state of Android development. You've been warned.</p>
